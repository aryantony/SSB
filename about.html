About Us

About Us

The Integrated SSB Virtuosos, a unit of CS Joint Services Academy (CSJSA), was was setup on 04 July 2021 with a goal to shape India’s youth and fuel their aspirations to join Indian Armed Forces in the Officer Cadre.
We are not a mere academy providing services, we are a community that supports and encourages students. Our founder personally guides all the students in their SSB preparation so that their dream of joining the Indian Armed Forces as commissioned officers can come true. Our sessions focus on psychological aspects of and the genesis of assessment at the Services Selection Board and methods to improve oneself. Lt Cdr Nikhil’s massive experience of assessing about 12500 candidates appearing for SSB has led to immense popularity among the students and teachers as well. We hope to create a niche for CSJSA so that every potential young aspiring student is able to fulfil the dream of serving as Officer in the Armed Forces
At CSJSA, we mentor defence aspirants and prepare them for exceling in the Services Selection Board Testing procedure to join the Indian Armed Forces as Officers. Our philosophy is deeply rooted in our ancient tradition of Manasa-Vacha-Karmana which means the alignment of mind, speech and actions. Alignment is mastered when the orientation of the personality is in a perfect balance or poise. It happens when the mind, speech and actions complement each other in a symmetric manner. This Sanskrit phrase is the gist of everything that the CSJSA strives to do. We mentor the students in such a way that there is perfect balance in their personality through the interplay of mind, speech and actions. When these three are in harmony, everything can be achieved, let alone the SSB. That’s our motto

The Founder

Lt. Commander Nikhil Kumar Chandrakala (Retd.)
Trained at “Defence Institute of Psychological Research” and erstwhile Naval Selection Board, Coimbatore :- a certified “Group Testing Officer” Youngest Panellist (Group Testing Officer) at the Services Selection Board of the Indian Armed Forces since 1947! The Founding Member and the 1st Group Testing Officer of SSB (Kolkata) – Indian Navy’s Fourth Officers’ Selection Board setup at Diamond Harbour, South 24 Paraganas, West Bengal. Served as Group Testing Officer at 12 SSB Bangalore, Selection Centre South – India’s First Defence Officers’ Selection Centre setup on 01 April 1949!
Studied B. Tech. Electrical Engineering from NIT Srinagar with an exemplary track record. Masters in Psychology with minors in Industrial and Organisational Psychology in First Class. Masters in Mobility Engineering from Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore. Cleared SSB at a young age and topped the exam for the Indian Army and the Indian Navy. Secured AIR 1 in the merit list.
Joined the Indian Navy in the Executive Branch (General Service Cadre) and served onboard 1971 war legacy warship INS Prabal, Flag Lieutenant to Flag Officer Commanding Maharashtra and Gujarat Naval Area, Captain of a Indian Naval Immediate Support Vessel T-15, Squadron Commander 81st ISV Squadron, Senior Officer ISVs (West). Vast experience in mentorship, career guidance, personality assessment and development, soft skills development. Experience of assessing more than 12500 candidates appearing for the SSB.
